Welcome to iziMoney

Scrape. Analyze. Profit.
Usage:
  • Check out the iziMoney Playground to interact with the model and get instant analysis of text that you type or paste.
  • Head over to the Requests tab and check out requests published by other users.
  • Create an account to submit your own requests, and watch the scraper scrape and the model predict.
iziMoney can help you scrape news articles from Google on any search keyword you choose and analyze the contents for financial sentiment.
The app uses Selenium to collect data and a Neural Network written with Keras, designed to excess with text and financial sentences.

PERIODS - number of periods the scraper will collect. A period is set by the scraper in the Tools tab of Google search.

PERIODICITY - the date range of a period. 5 day periodicity would collect articles published within 5 days after the period start.

RESULTS PAGES - determines the number of search pages the scraper will collect per period.
